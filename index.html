What Is AssignMind?

Plain and simple, we help students stay on track while browsing the net. If we notice you go to a "trouble maker" site (Facebook, Reddit, ect.) we will send you a push notification notifying you that there's still work to be done, money to be made, and worlds to conqure. Better yet, our push notifications gracefully link back to your worksite of choice. We are the ultimate hustler's tool.
